Presentation Letter

Karate-Braga

Dear friends

In 2026, Karate Braga proudly celebrates its 25th anniversary! To mark this historic milestone, this international event is dedicated to commemorating 25 years of our dojo's existence.

The International Budo Karate Seminar returns to Braga on the 5th, 6th, and 7th of June 2026, for the third time in Portugal, in a special edition consisting of three days of intensive training. This event will feature the presence of Sensei André Bertel, a world-renowned Karate instructor who will travel especially from Japan for this unique celebration.

To celebrate the club's 25th anniversary, the registration fee is both unique and symbolic for all participants, whether national or international: just 25 euros – a commemorative price for Karate Braga's 25th anniversary. There are no special prices or discounts, as this celebration is for everyone!

Responding to the wishes of many participants, we will once again organise on Saturday a social dinner open to all practitioners and their companions. The aim is to strengthen bonds of friendship in a relaxed atmosphere with Sensei André Bertel, including lively moments of "karatekas at karaoke" (KK).

Both the Seminar and the Social Dinner are open to all karate practitioners, from any club or association, regardless of affiliation.

In this special edition, we celebrate dedication to training, the sharing of knowledge, and the continual pursuit of development and improvement that unites us.

It is a unique opportunity to experience and deepen the true spirit of Karate-Do, integrating past, present, and future—now made even more meaningful by our dojo's anniversary.

We count on everyone to make this moment even more special. The presence of Sensei André Bertel, a former direct student of Tetsuhiko Asai Sensei (1935-2006), enriches this celebration further. His teaching maintains a unique connection to traditional Budo and true Shotokan, recognised and respected worldwide.

We would also like to thank Braga City Council, the Union of the Parishes of Real, Dume, and Semelhe, as well as all sponsors and supporting organisations, without whom this special anniversary event would not be possible.

We hope to exceed everyone's expectations and provide a memorable experience. Together, let us make this 25th anniversary seminar a milestone on all our karate journeys.


Karate Braga

Francisco Pinto
