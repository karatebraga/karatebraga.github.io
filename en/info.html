Presentation Letter

Karate-Braga

Dear Friends

In 2026, Karate Braga proudly celebrates its 25th anniversary! To mark this historic milestone, this international event is part of the celebrations of 25 years of existence of this club.

The International Seminar Budo Karate returns to Portugal on 5, 6 and 7 June 2026, for the third time in Braga, in a special three-day edition of intensive training. This event will feature Sensei André Bertel, a world reference in Karate.

In response to the wishes of many participants, we will again organise a social dinner on Saturday open to all practitioners and companions. The aim is to strengthen bonds of friendship in a relaxed atmosphere with Sensei André Bertel.

This seminar is a unique opportunity to experience and deepen the true spirit of Karate-Do, integrating past, present and future, now even more meaningful with our club's anniversary.

The presence of Sensei André Bertel, a former direct student of Tetsuhiko Asai Sensei (1935-2006), enriches our knowledge and maintains a unique link to traditional Budo and true Shotokan, recognised and respected worldwide.

We thank the Municipality of Braga, the Union of the Parishes of Real, Dume and Semelhe, as well as all sponsors and all entities and individuals involved, without whom this event would not be possible.

We hope to exceed everyone's expectations and provide a memorable experience. Together, we will make this 3rd International Budo Karate Seminar and our club's 25th anniversary a defining moment on our path in Karate.

Karate Braga

Francisco Pinto
