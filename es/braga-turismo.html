Braga is a magnificent city. Come and visit Braga!




Touristic Circuit "Braga and Bom-Jesus TOUR":


  • Starting at Avenida Central, this circuit runs through the main points of interest in Braga and lasts approximately one hour and 15 minutes, with six daily departures, between 09.45 hours and 17.30 hours.
  • On the route there is a stop near the Tourist Office and a longer stop, about 10 minutes in Bom Jesus. The circuit also allows you to connect to Bom Jesus cable railway, the central bus station and the central train station.
  • This service has an audio system, available in six languages: Portuguese, English, Spanish, German, French and Italian.
  • The ticket price is € 12 for adults and € 6 for children. Groups with 4 or more people enjoy a 10% discount. Tickets can be purchased on board at the agents identified for this purpose.
